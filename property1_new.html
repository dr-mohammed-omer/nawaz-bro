Luxury Villa in Banjara Hills

Banjara Hills, Hyderabad, Telangana, India

₹2.5 Cr

Property Description

Welcome to this exquisite luxury villa nestled in the prestigious Banjara Hills area of Hyderabad. This magnificent property offers a perfect blend of modern architecture and elegant design, creating an ideal living space for those who appreciate the finer things in life.

Spanning across 3500 square feet, this 4-bedroom villa features high ceilings, large windows that allow ample natural light, and premium finishes throughout. The property is surrounded by lush landscaped gardens, providing a serene and private environment.

The ground floor comprises a spacious living room, a formal dining area, a modern kitchen with high-end appliances, and a guest bedroom. The upper floor houses the master suite with a walk-in closet and a luxurious bathroom, along with two additional bedrooms.

Additional amenities include a home theater, a swimming pool, a gym, and a two-car garage. The property is equipped with smart home technology, allowing you to control lighting, temperature, and security systems with ease.
