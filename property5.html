Property Description

This beautiful garden villa in Kompally offers a perfect blend of modern comfort and natural beauty. Set on a 5000 sq.ft plot, this 4-bedroom villa spans 3000 sq.ft of built-up area and features a lush garden that surrounds the property.

The villa showcases a contemporary design with spacious interiors and large windows that bring in abundant natural light. The ground floor features a welcoming living room, a formal dining area, a modern kitchen, and a guest bedroom. The upper floor houses three bedrooms, including the master suite with an attached balcony overlooking the garden.

The highlight of this property is its beautifully landscaped garden with mature trees, flowering plants, and a small vegetable patch. The outdoor space also includes a covered patio perfect for al fresco dining and entertaining.

The villa is equipped with modern amenities, including air conditioning in all bedrooms, solar water heating, rainwater harvesting, and a power backup system. The property also includes a two-car garage and additional parking space in the driveway.
